## The 4-Step Workflow (Do This Every Time)

### Step 1: SERP Research (2 searches)
```
Search 1: "[keyword] complete guide 2026"
Search 2: "[keyword] [related term] [expert source]"
```
Scan the top 5-10 results. Ask: **What do they ALL miss?**

### Step 2: Gap Analysis (answer these 4 questions)
1. What specific angle, data point, or use case does NO top-ranking article cover?
2. What do readers actually want to know that's not in the results?
3. What authoritative source (AKC, VCA, ASPCA, Cornell, ADA, etc.) can anchor the article?
4. What's the one comparison table no one has built for this topic?

### Step 3: Write (use the structure below)
### Step 4: QA Check (run down the checklist at bottom)

## Hard Rules (NEVER Violate)

### Paragraph Rule
- **HARD LIMIT: 50 words per paragraph**
- 2-3 sentences max
- One-sentence paragraphs are encouraged
- Break anything over 50 words into two paragraphs

### Banned Words (never use these)
delve, tapestry, landscape, crucial, leverage, utilize, cutting-edge, game-changer,
revolutionize, seamless, robust, furthermore, moreover, realm, symphony, bustling,
innovative, uncover

### Banned Intro Phrases (never start with these)
- "In today's..."
- "When it comes to..."
- "If you're looking for..."
- "Are you tired of..."
- "In this article..."
- "Have you ever wondered..."

### No Fabricated Data
- Only verifiable facts from real sources
- Real organizations: AKC, AVMA, VCA, ASPCA, ADA, Cornell, Harvard, etc.
- Real people: cite by name + credential + year
- Never invent studies, statistics, or quotes
- Use hedged language when uncertain: "veterinarians commonly recommend..." / "most studies suggest..."

## Gap Analysis Patterns That Win (Proven From Session 1)

| Pattern | Example Used In |
|---------|----------------|
| **"True X vs. Simulated X"** — most readers diagnose wrong | Separation Anxiety (true SA vs. boredom SA) |
| **"X as #1 intervention nobody does first"** — the counterintuitive anchor | Joint Health (weight loss > all supplements) |
| **"The Y problem nobody mentions"** — environmental factor | Joint Health (slippery floors), Car Safety (parked car temp) |
| **"Back-to-office / modern context"** — new trigger most guides miss | Separation Anxiety (remote work transition) |
| **"Size/dose specificity"** — give exact numbers, not "consult your vet" | All articles (6.1% weight loss = measurable improvement) |
| **"Certification/standard explained"** — decode what labels mean | Car Safety (CPS crash test vs. "crash-tested" marketing) |
| **"Decision table"** — when X vs. when Y | Crate Training (wire vs. plastic vs. soft-sided) |
